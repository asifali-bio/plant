🔬 Interactive Visualizations

This page hosts interactive Plotly visualizations exported as standalone HTML files. Click any visualization below to open it directly in your browser.

⚠️ Note: These are large interactive files (5–10 MB). Performance depends on your system and browser.

🧬 GO (Gene Ontology) Visualizations

GO Rainbow 1

Species labeled directly on the x-axis with unique rainbow colors.

GO Rainbow 2

Species shown in a legend; rainbow color mapping per species.

GO Viridis

Perceptually uniform viridis scale (yellow → dark blue). Dark blue highlights differentiation; yellow highlights shared patterns.

🧪 Pfam Visualizations

Pfam Rainbow 1

Species labeled directly on the x-axis with rainbow coloring.

Pfam Rainbow 2

Species displayed in a legend with rainbow mapping.

Pfam Viridis

Viridis gradient (yellow → green → blue), resembling diffusion patterns. Ordering reflects the underlying R workflow.

🎨 Notes on Color Design

  • Viridis emphasizes quantitative structure and perceptual uniformity
  • Recommended for accurate gradient interpretation

⚙️ Performance Notes

  • GPU-accelerated rendering via WebGL
  • Best performance with ≥16 GB RAM
  • Lower-memory systems may experience slowdowns

If you encounter issues:

  • Open one visualization at a time
  • Close other browser tabs
  • Use Chrome or Firefox
